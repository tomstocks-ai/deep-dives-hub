## Gists

### CRCL — Circle Internet Group · <span class="rating-buy">BUY</span> · $106.36

Circle is the issuer of USDC, the second-largest stablecoin with ~$75B in circulation and ~25% market share. Revenue of $2.75B in FY2025 (+64% YoY) comes almost entirely from interest on Treasury-backed reserves managed by BlackRock. IPO'd June 2025 at $31, peaked ~$299, now trading ~$106 after a sharp +25% rally from April lows. RSI at 66.95 is near overbought, suggesting near-term consolidation risk. The GENIUS Act (stablecoin regulation) is the key catalyst — passage would solidify Circle's regulatory moat and pressure unregulated competitors like Tether. The biggest structural concern is Coinbase's revenue-sharing agreement, which captures ~56% of total reserve revenue. Interest rate sensitivity is extreme: every 1% Fed move = ~$680M revenue swing.

**Bull:** $180–$220 · **Base:** $100–$130 · **Bear:** $45–$65
