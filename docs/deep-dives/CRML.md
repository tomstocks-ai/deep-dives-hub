## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Critical Metals Corp is a pre-revenue rare earth mining company developing the Tanbreez Project in Greenland - one of the world's largest known rare earth deposits. The company has made significant strategic progress with JVs in Saudi Arabia ($1.5B) and Romania, plus critical partnerships for Western rare earth supply chains. ## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Business Model
Critical Metals Corp is a mining exploration and development company focused on critical minerals, with two primary assets:

**Primary Asset: Tanbreez Project (Greenland)**
- One of the world's largest known rare earth deposits
- **4.7 billion-tonne multi-element resource** (S-K 1300 conversion underway)
- **27% Heavy Rare Earth Elements (HREE)** - significantly higher than industry standard
- Located in Southern Greenland with year-round access
- Exploitation license granted by Greenland Government in **2020**
- Current ownership: **42% CRML, 7.5% European Lithium, remaining with original owners**

**Secondary Asset: Wolfsberg Lithium Project (Austria)**
- Lithium exploration project
- Austrian Government extended mining license (January 2026)
- Strategic for European lithium supply chain

### 1.2 Tanbreez Project Technical Details
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Resource | 4.7 billion tonnes |
| Heavy REE Content | 27% of total REE |
| Light REE Content | 73% of total REE |
| Mining License | Granted 2020 (valid) |
| Drilling Database | 414 drill holes, 366,000+ assays |
| Bulk Testing | ~709 tonnes completed |
| Mine Life Potential | 100+ years |

**Strategic Advantage:** The 27% HREE content is a critical differentiator. Heavy rare earths command premium pricing and are essential for military-grade systems, advanced magnets, and precision electronics - areas where Western supply chains are critically dependent on China.
